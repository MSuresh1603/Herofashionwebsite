Operations

The biggest advantage of Herofashion is that we are vertically integrated from knitting to finished garment. The quality policy of Herofashion strives to achieve customer delight by supplying organic clothing in nature’s own way through traceability from farming to finished product.

 

Quality checks by our QC team are done in all stages: inspection of grey yarn, knitted fabric, dyeing, printing, embroidery and garmenting.

Spinning

PERFORMANCE through Spinning

We are associated with south India’s leading spinning mills with abundant resources and production capacities.

Knitting & Weaving

PERFORMANCE through Knitting

We are well equipped with advanced high speed circular and flat knit machines producing fabrics ranging from jersey, interlock, ribs and many more designs. Almost all our knitting machines are imported from renowned makers facilitating Hero to exceed the expectations of customers offering superior quality fabric.

For woven, our supply chain has refined auto looms for making dobby and jacquard weaves.

Dyeing & Finishing

PERFORMANCE through Dyeing

We have tie-up with well Established Dyers with sophisticated European machines that have capability to dye and finish fine fabrics.

Care is extended such that only GOTS approved ECO friendly dyes are used. The effluents are treated in R.O units, ensuring a cleaner environment. Plus the dyeing facility fulfils all pollution treatment norms as it’s duly approved by PCB (pollution control board)

Printing

PERFORMANCE through Dyeing

We have very good support systems of printing which comprises of both table and machine printing technique. with potential capacity of 10,000 pcs per day and Use AZO free ECO friendly dyes and chemicals which are approved by GOTS and are free from phthalates, lead and PVC.

We have capacity of machines to deliver pigments, non-PVC, flock, transfers, reactive and discharge print in myriad colours. To remove unwanted odour and ensure proper fixation, curing and fusing are perfectly accomplised with the aid of modern machine.

We can accomplish a potential of 10,000 pcs a day.

Embroidery

PERFORMANCE through Dyeing

We carry out many varieties of fashionable embroidery as per customer’s specification. Our automatic high speed Tajima machines that are capable of making any kind of embroidery applique and badges.

Garment Production

PERFORMANCE through Dyeing

From cutting to garmenting, we have all kinds of machineries to produce any style of garment. Our fabric cutting machine from F.K, Italy is fully automated which is able to cut 18,000 pcs a day.

The production lines in factory are installed with all types of sewing machines and are capable of producing 20,000 pcs a day. We lay a great emphasis on quality consciousness and all in-line and final inspections are done across departments as per global standards.
