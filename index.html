Welcome to
Hero Fashion

PERFORMANCE through PERFECTION

Established in the year 1989 with a vision to achieve excellence in product and process. We are one of the leading exporters of knitted and woven apparels from tirupur, textile hub of South India. With a perfect blend of skilled workforce, technology and infrastructure we are able to deliver quality products meeting required standards and specifications of the customer.

 

 

Hero believes in 3P’s – Product, People and Planet.

We focus on creating high quality, responsibly produced garment with a people friendly team in a safe and healthy work environment, conserving energy and natural resources, being earth positive along the way.
