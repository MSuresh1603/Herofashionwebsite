About
Hero Fashion

PERFORMANCE through PERFECTION

As the seed of entrepreneurship in him sprouted, Mr.Sundaramurthy founded Hero Fashion at the young age of 22. Since then, sternly following his vision of adhering to unrelenting quality has lead Hero Fashion to grow from an organization with handful of employees to one of the leading exporters of knitting products from Tirupur.

 

 

Under his able guidance, embarking on a mission to contribute to a sustainable future, with an amalgamation of skilled workforce, technology and infrastructure, Hero Fashion has evolved into manufacturing and exporting eco-friendly organic garments.

 

 

Exemplifying his mettle as an entrepreneur, among the industry peers and customers all around the globe Hero Fashion has earned a reputation for delivering supreme quality products that meets the required standards and specifications of the customer.
